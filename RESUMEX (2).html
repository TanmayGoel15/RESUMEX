About

Built by Students,
For Job Seekers

ResumeX is a smart resume builder developed as a project by students at VIT, combining modern web technologies with intelligent ATS analysis to help applicants get noticed.

We built this because we believe every job seeker deserves a professional, ATS-ready resume — regardless of design skills.
